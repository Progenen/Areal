GOOD FOR PEOPLE & THE PLANET

We are committed to social good. Our impact starts with the products we make and the way we make them. That means providing quality ingredients for healthy aging while maintaining better practicies to protect our planet. Our aim is to support sustainable livelihoods for all.

GOOD FOR PEOPLE

Call us old-fashioned, but we believe in nurturing relationships. That's why we take an interest in knowing the people behind our ingredients. We work directly with our partner farmers and processor communities to ensure fair labor practices for better livelihoods, living wages and needs-based empowerment programs.

ENCOURAGING BETTER NUTRITION

We are on a mission to help nourish healthy aging with high quality ingredients and scienifically-backed recipes. We're proud to elevate and support better nutrition with great tasting products that empower you to feel your best from the very first sip.

DAIRY INITIATIVES

We are making waves for sustainable farming and are commited to improving the farming landscape for years to come. By supporting fair trade with our growing dairy farmer ambassador program known as the Net Zero Dairy Initiative, we are nurturing local communities and sourcing ingredients responsibly. We support the rights of every worker and employee across our value chain while inspiring others by sharing best practice across the industry.

We’re committed to putting people and planet before product

And now we’ve made it official by undertaking our journey to become a B-Corp.

GOOD FOR THE PLANET

We are balancing our emissions, reducing waste, offsetting when necessary and choosing renewable energy sources to help protect the planet's precious resources because we think the planet deserves to age well too.

100% of our sites are zero waste to landfill

REGENERATIVE PRACTICES

By 2025 our plan is to source 20% of our key ingredients through regenerative agriculture methods. With this rehabilitative approach, we are doing our part to strengthen the health and vitality of the environment while maintaining the highest quality ingredients.
